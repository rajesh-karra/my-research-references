## Earth and Climate AI Vision
Protecting nature is a data, modeling, and optimization challenge. The pathway combines mathematics and AI for measurable impact.

### Math to Impact Mapping
| Mathematics Topic | Environmental Application |
|---|---|
| PDEs | Atmospheric and ocean dynamics modeling |
| Optimization | Carbon-aware logistics, renewable placement |
| Probability and Statistics | Extreme weather risk estimation |
| Dynamical Systems | Ecosystem stability and species modeling |
| Linear Algebra | Satellite tensor data processing |

### AI Systems for Environmental Protection
- Computer vision for deforestation and biodiversity monitoring.
- Sequence models for flood and weather forecasting.
- Reinforcement learning for smart-grid balancing.
- LLMs for climate literature synthesis and policy gap analysis.

### High-Impact Applied Directions
- Precision agriculture and water optimization.
- Smart renewable energy grids.
- Bioacoustic anti-poaching detection.
- Digital twins of Earth for scenario simulations.
