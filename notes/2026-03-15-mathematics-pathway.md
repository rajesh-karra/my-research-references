# Mathematics Learning Pathway: Foundations to University

Date: 2026-03-15
Track: Mathematics for AI and Quantum Computing

## Goal
Build rigorous math foundations that directly support AI, ML, and Quantum AI research.

## Phase 1: Middle School Foundation (Standards 6-10)
| Concept Area | Core Topics | Base Resources |
|---|---|---|
| Arithmetic and Number Systems | Integers, fractions, decimals, ratio, roots | NCERT Textbooks I-XII |
| Algebra | Expressions, equations, polynomials, quadratics | NCERT + Algebra practice resources |
| Geometry | Angles, triangles, circles, area, volume | NCERT geometry sequence |
| Trigonometry | Intro trig, identities, heights and distances | NCERT Class 10 + trig practice |

## Phase 2: High School and Pre-University (Standards 11-12)
| Concept Area | Core Topics | AP Equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| Calculus | Limits, derivatives, integrals, differential equations | AP Calculus AB/BC |
| Algebra | Complex numbers, sequences, matrices, determinants | AP Precalculus functions and modeling |
| Vectors and 3D | Vector algebra, lines, planes | AP Physics vectors support |
| Probability and Statistics | Random variables, Bayes theorem, distributions | AP Statistics |

## Phase 3: University and AI/ML Core
| Module | Topics | Recommended Resources |
|---|---|---|
| Linear Algebra | Eigenvalues/eigenvectors, transforms, PCA | 3Blue1Brown, Mathematics for AI notes |
| Multivariate Calculus | Gradient, Jacobian, Hessian, optimization | MIT OCW, calculus visualized resources |
| Probability and Statistics | CLT, MLE, hypothesis testing, Bayesian methods | textbooks + applied problem sets |
| Discrete Mathematics | Set theory, logic, graph theory, number theory | MIT Mathematics for CS |

## Recommended Study Path
1. Start with Khan Academy AP Calculus BC pathway (includes AB scope).
2. Use OpenStax chapters for deep reading.
3. Use AP CED checklists to track topic coverage.
4. Solve weekly problem sets and maintain error logs.

## Suggested Timeline Options
- 3-month sprint: fast-paced with daily problem solving.
- 6-month deep track: balanced with concept reviews and projects.

## Deliverables
- Weekly solved problem notebook.
- Monthly concept summary note.
- Quarterly assessment and revision plan.
